About Me

Hello! My name is Bethani. My interest in Technology began in 2014 when I found a passion for 3D modelling using software such as Blender and ZBrush. Through this, I had pursued my interests in sharing creativity, problem-solving and setting challenges for myself. I naturally found myself wanting to learn everything there is to know about coding!

Finding SheCodes and being a full-time coding student has given me the opportunity to learn more about coding in a fun, inspiring way within a very supportive community. My current focus is continuing to build knowledge and experience of coding as well as beginning freelance work.
