Hello! My name is Bethani. My interest in Technology began in 2014 when I found a passion for 3D modelling using software such as Blender and ZBrush. Through becoming a part of this community, I found a love for sharing creativity, problem-solving and setting challenges for myself. I naturally found myself learning HTML & CSS to create my own designs and bring them to life on a once blank webpage.


Through SheCodes, I had an opportunity to learn more about coding in a fun, inspiring way within a very supportive community. My current focus is continuing to build knowledge and experience of coding.
